Throughout its history, the Old Basement Church has been more than just a place of worship; it has been a
cornerstone of the community. During difficult times, such as economic hardships and periods of social upheaval,
the church's basement served as a refuge and support center for locals. It hosted food drives, shelter for the homeless,
and community gatherings that fostered a sense of unity and resilience. The church role in providing both spiritual and practical
support highlights its importance beyond religious functions
